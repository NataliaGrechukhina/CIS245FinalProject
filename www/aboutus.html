About Us

Lotus company was founded in 2010 and it is a professional team of creative people that incarnating the dreams of the client with the help of flowers and decor.

Lotus not only sells flowers, with us you will see interesting, daring solutions in different styles, colors and themes for any event. Our flower company focused on long-term cooperation with our clients and provide best quality.

Each design of the event is very individual and depends on many factors: the number of flowers, their varieties, seasonality, the complexity of the design decision and many others. Therefore, our experience plus your wishes and financial possibilities, as a result will create a harmonious space for your celebration.

High professionalism of Lotus team allows us to carry out a wide variety of projects and solve complex problems facing clients in the field of flower design.

For 11 years of successful work, more than 80,000 people have become happy clients of Lotus. The best reward for us is the happy faces of our clients, their relatives and friends, as well as sincere feedback on the work of our company.
